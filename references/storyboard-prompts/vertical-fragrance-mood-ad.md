# Vertical Fragrance Mood Ad Pattern

Use this pattern for 9:16 vertical luxury fragrance, skincare, beauty, and quiet mood ads where atmosphere and texture matter more than plot.

## Best For

- vertical fragrance ads
- skincare mood films
- beauty ritual clips
- UGC-style premium brand ads
- 15-second atmospheric commercials
- quiet, soft, high-end visual campaigns

## Core Workflow

1. Use GPT Image 2 to generate a 12-shot vertical mood storyboard poster.
2. Upload the storyboard into Seedance 2.0.
3. Generate a 15-second 9:16 video.
4. Finish in editing software if needed:
   - replace soft ambient music
   - fine-tune color and texture
   - upscale / enhance resolution

## Image2 Storyboard Board

Use:

- vertical 9:16 layout
- 3 columns x 4 rows
- 12 shots
- clean premium paper texture
- brand title at top
- product name and tagline
- each panel: cinematic image + short description

## Mood Ad Structure

For fragrance:

1. first light / atmosphere establish
2. stillness / breathing moment
3. product appears
4. mist begins
5. scent spreads
6. enters emotional space
7. body/fabric relaxes
8. negative space / pause
9. product lock
10. model eye lift
11. subject and space merge
12. hero product shot

Adapt the shot titles to the brand concept.

## Visual Style

Specify:

- soft white / pale gray / misty purple / cool morning light
- sheer curtains
- flowers or brand motif
- perfume mist
- glass highlights
- quiet negative space
- high-end fragrance ad texture

Avoid:

- e-commerce short video feel
- over-warm yellow filter
- hard sell graphics
- heavy subtitles
- fantasy effects
- cheap studio portrait look

## Reusable Image2 Instruction Block

```text
请创作一张高端香水/美妆广告「氛围篇」分镜海报，竖版 9:16，3列 × 4行，共12个镜头。

整体为干净白色或柔和浅色背景，轻微高级纸面纹理，顶部居中放品牌名、英文名、产品类型和一句标语。中间是12格分镜，每格上半部分为电影感画面，下半部分为简短中文分镜说明。

整体像真正的高端品牌广告 storyboard，不是普通拼图。画面要安静、通透、柔和、有留白，产品、人物、光线、材质和香雾必须保持一致。
```

## Reusable Seedance Prompt

```text
请根据这张高端香水/美妆广告分镜图，生成一支完整的氛围篇广告短片，时长约15秒，竖版9:16。

请保持分镜图中的镜头顺序、产品外观、模特气质和整体色调。视频依次表现：[按12个镜头顺序概括动作]。

镜头语言：慢推近、轻微横移、柔焦背景、浅景深、产品/肌肤/玻璃高光、柔和粒子、自然光影。

不要字幕，不要分镜框，不要海报排版，不要暖黄滤镜，不要电商短视频感，不要夸张特效。
```

## When To Use Instead Of Luxury Ad Creative Direction Board

Use this pattern when:

- the ad is vertical 9:16
- the campaign is mainly mood/texture/ritual
- the user wants a fast 15-second result
- detailed route maps and camera positions are unnecessary

Use `luxury-ad-creative-direction-board.md` when:

- the project needs full campaign planning
- there are route guides, model movement paths, camera positions, and 8-scene premium board sections
