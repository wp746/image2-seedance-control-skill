# Scene Continuity Board Pattern

Use this pattern when one scene is complex enough that scene layout, character blocking, and emotional continuity need a separate board beyond the basic scene asset board.

## Applicability

Use when:
- multiple characters interact in the same space across shots
- characters move through the space with specific paths
- emotional state changes as characters enter/exit the scene
- the scene has multiple camera positions that must stay consistent

Do not use for:
- simple single-character scenes with one fixed camera
- scenes already covered by a basic scene asset board + storyboard

## Core Pattern

The Scene Continuity Board is a bridge between the Scene Asset Board (what the space looks like) and the Storyboard (how the camera shoots it). It locks character placement, movement, and emotional flow within the physical space.

Must include:

- **Same location from several useful angles**: pick 2-3 angles that show the space as characters will experience it (not just empty establishing shots)
- **Character positions and movement path**: mark starting positions, movement arrows with dotted lines, and key blocking moments (where they stop, turn, sit, interact)
- **Light source and atmosphere**: consistent with the scene asset board, but show how characters are lit from different positions
- **Key props and where they sit**: which objects characters touch, pick up, put down, or move past
- **Emotional state entering and leaving the scene**: what the character feels when entering vs. exiting, and how the space contributes to that change
- **Edit-in and edit-out frames**: the exact frame that starts and ends this scene, so the next scene or segment can hand off cleanly

## Relationship to Other Boards

```
Scene Asset Board     → "This is what the space looks like"
Scene Continuity Board → "This is how characters occupy and move through the space"
Storyboard            → "This is how the camera shoots that movement"
```

The continuity board should not replace the scene asset board or the storyboard. It adds the character-space relationship layer.

## When to Skip

Skip this board and use only the scene asset board when:
- the scene is a simple establishing shot
- only one character in a static position
- the storyboard already includes a floor plan with character positions

## Reusable Instruction Block

```text
这是场景连续性板，连接"场景长什么样"和"摄影机怎么拍"。它不是场景资产板（不展示空场景），也不是故事板（不展示逐镜头）。它专门锁定角色在空间中的位置、移动和情绪流动。

必须包含：
- SCENE_01 的多个可用角度（展示角色会体验到的空间视角，不只是空场景）
- 角色起始位置和移动路径：用虚线箭头标注
- 关键阻挡位：角色在哪里停下、转身、坐下、互动
- 光源方向和氛围：与场景资产板一致，但展示角色在不同位置的光照
- 关键道具和位置：哪些道具会被角色触碰、拿起、放下、经过
- 进入和离开场景的情绪状态
- 剪辑入点和出点画面

版式：导演和摄影指导用的连续性检查板。文字大而清楚，空间和人物关系一目了然。
```
